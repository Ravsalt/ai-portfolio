Who Am I?

Started coding at 13 just to see what would break. Turns out, I’m still doing that—just now, I also fix it. Now I'm deep in development, automation, and AI, mostly to make my life easier (and maybe yours too).

I've built websites, tools, and backend systems, sometimes because I had to, other times just to prove a point. Tutorials? Yeah, I’ve been there, spent some time in “tutorial hell” too, but hey, that’s how you learn, right? Honestly, I still don’t know what I’m doing half the time, but I make it work anyway.

I don’t just write code; I make things run faster, smarter, and with fewer bugs (hopefully).

Now diving into AI & ML because, let’s be real, robots are taking over, and I’d rather be the one coding them than getting replaced by them.
